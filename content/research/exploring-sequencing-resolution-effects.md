**Winnie Xu**, [Francis Nguyen](https://twitter.com/acatgatatatac), [Michael Hoffman](https://hoffmanlab.org/)

We validated the utility of two novel next-generation-sequencing assays (ChIP-Exo and ChIP-Nexus) in epigenetic exploration of cancer transcriptomes. I implemented custom bioinformatics pipelines, unsupervised ML algorithms ([Segway](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3340533/)), and the [stable marriage](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stable_marriage_problem) algorithm.

Princess Margaret Cancer Research Center 2018.
